\section{Holographic Entanglement Entropy}
The Ryu-Takayanagi formula relates the entanglement entropy $S_A$ to the area of a minimal surface $\gamma_A$:
\begin{equation}
S_A = \frac{\text{Area}(\gamma_A)}{4G_N}
\end{equation}
