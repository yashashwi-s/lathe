\begin{abstract}
In this paper we examine the bi-Hamiltonian structure of the generalized KdV-hierarchies. We verify that both Hamiltonian structures take the form of Kirillov brackets on the Kac-Moody algebra, and that they define a coordinated system. Classical extended conformal algebras are obtained from the second Poisson bracket. In particular, we construct the \$W\_n\textasciicircum{}l\$ algebras, first discussed for the case \$n=3\$ and \$l=2\$ by A. Polyakov and M. Bershadsky.
\end{abstract}
