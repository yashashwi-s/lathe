\begin{abstract}
We extend Felder's construction of Fock space resolutions for the Virasoro minimal models to all irreducible modules with \$c\textbackslash{}leq 1\$. In particular, we provide resolutions for the representations corresponding to the boundary and exterior of the Kac table.
\end{abstract}
