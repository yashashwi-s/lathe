\begin{abstract}
We propose possible new string theories based on local world-sheet symmetries corresponding to extensions of the Virasoro algebra by fractional spin currents. They have critical central charges \$c=6(K+8)/(K+2)\$ and Minkowski space-time dimensions \$D=2+16/K\$ for \$K\textbackslash{}geq2\$ an integer. We present evidence for their existence by constructing modular invariant partition functions and the massless particle spectra. The dimension \$4\$ and \$6\$ strings have space-time supersymmetry.
\end{abstract}
