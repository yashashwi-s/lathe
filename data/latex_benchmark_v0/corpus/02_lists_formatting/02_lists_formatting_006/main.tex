\item[Fig.1]
Plot of the region in parameter space $(a, b)$ allowed by the
inflationary conditions in the text. The solid lines represent
those bounds
which do not depend on the scale $M$ of the phase transition.
The dashed curve correspond to the bounds associated with the
scale $M=10^{11}$ GeV and the dotted curve to those related to
$M=10^{16}$ GeV. The allowed region is the one below the curves.
The border $b=0$ is excluded from it.
We have labelled the curves as follows: QG corresponds to the
condition associated to neglecting quantum gravity effects,
$\Phi$ corresponds to the condition for an increasing scalar
field, RW corresponds to the bound on isotropy and
homogeneity at the time of nucleosynthesis and pN--NS
corresponds to the bounds from post--Newtonian experiments and
the nucleosynthesis bound on $\omega$.

\end{description}
