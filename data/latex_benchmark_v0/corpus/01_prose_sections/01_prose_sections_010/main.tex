\begin{abstract}
We bosonise the complex-boson realisations of the \$W\_\textbackslash{}infty\$ and \$W\_\{1+\textbackslash{}infty\}\$ algebras. We obtain nonlinear realisations of \$W\_\textbackslash{}infty\$ and \$W\_\{1+\textbackslash{}infty\}\$ in terms of a pair of fermions and a real scalar. By further bosonising the fermions, we then obtain realisations of \$W\_\textbackslash{}infty\$ in terms of two scalars. Keeping the most non-linear terms in the scalars only, we arrive at two-scalar realisations of classical \$w\_\textbackslash{}infty\$.
\end{abstract}
