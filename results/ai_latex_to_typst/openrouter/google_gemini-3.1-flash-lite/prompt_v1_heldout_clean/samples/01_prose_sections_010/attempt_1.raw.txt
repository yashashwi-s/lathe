#block(inset: (x: 2em), [
  *Abstract*\
  We bosonise the complex-boson realisations of the $W_infinity$ and $W_(1+infinity)$ algebras. We obtain nonlinear realisations of $W_infinity$ and $W_(1+infinity)$ in terms of a pair of fermions and a real scalar. By further bosonising the fermions, we then obtain realisations of $W_infinity$ in terms of two scalars. Keeping the most non-linear terms in the scalars only, we arrive at two-scalar realisations of classical $w_infinity$.
])
